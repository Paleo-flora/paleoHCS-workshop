Central Topic

"A comprehensive review of the oceanographic, climatic, and ecological features of the Humboldt Current System (HCS) over the last 23 million years."

Confirmed Keynote Speakers

Frank Lamy

Paleoceanography

Providing expert insights into the long-term oceanographic evolution of the system.

Elisa Malinverno

Paleoecosystem Perspective

Focusing on Diatoms and the biological response to historical climate shifts.

Pierre Sepulchre

Modeller Perspective

Linking Ocean-Atmosphere dynamics, Andes uplift, and climate during the Miocene.
